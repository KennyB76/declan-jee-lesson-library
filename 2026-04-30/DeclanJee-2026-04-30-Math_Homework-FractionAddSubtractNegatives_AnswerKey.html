Problem 1
$\left(-\dfrac{1}{2}\right) + \left(-\dfrac{3}{4}\right)$
common denom
LCD $= 4$:  $-\dfrac{1}{2} = -\dfrac{2}{4}$
$-\dfrac{2}{4} + \left(-\dfrac{3}{4}\right) = -\dfrac{5}{4}$
$-\dfrac{5}{4}$
Problem 2
$\left(-\dfrac{5}{6}\right) - \left(-\dfrac{2}{3}\right)$
subtract negative = add
$-\dfrac{5}{6} + \dfrac{2}{3}$ (subtract a negative → add)
LCD $= 6$:  $\dfrac{2}{3} = \dfrac{4}{6}$
$-\dfrac{5}{6} + \dfrac{4}{6} = -\dfrac{1}{6}$
$-\dfrac{1}{6}$
