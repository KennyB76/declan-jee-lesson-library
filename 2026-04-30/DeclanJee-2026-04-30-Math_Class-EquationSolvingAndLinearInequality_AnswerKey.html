Part B · Linear Equations & Slope
Problem 6
Find the slope through $(2, 3)$ and $(6, 11)$.
slope formula
$m = \dfrac{y_2 - y_1}{x_2 - x_1} = \dfrac{11 - 3}{6 - 2} = \dfrac{8}{4} = 2$
$m = 2$
Problem 7
Slope $m = -2$, passes through $(1, 5)$. Write $y = mx + b$.
point-slope slope-intercept
Start with $y = -2x + b$
Plug in $(1, 5)$:   $5 = -2(1) + b \Rightarrow 5 = -2 + b \Rightarrow b = 7$
$y = -2x + 7$
Problem 8
$y = \dfrac{1}{2}x - 3$. Find slope, y-intercept, and $y$ when $x = 8$.
slope-intercept reading substitution
(a) Slope = coefficient of $x$ = $\dfrac{1}{2}$
(b) y-intercept = constant term = $-3$ → point $(0, -3)$
(c) $y = \dfrac{1}{2}(8) - 3 = 4 - 3 = 1$
slope $=\tfrac{1}{2}$,  b = $-3$,  $y(8) = 1$
